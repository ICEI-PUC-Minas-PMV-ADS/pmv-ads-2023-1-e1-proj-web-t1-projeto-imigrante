    Mental Healthy

    Immigrating involves experiencing stressful situations that pose a risk to mental health in relation to two factors: the vulnerability of the immigrant and the obstacles encountered in adapting to the host country. Find tips for staying mentally healthy and learn how to obtain free professional help.

    Social Assistance and Women

    In Brazil, Social Assistance is a right for all people, regardless of their nationality or migration status. Through it, it is possible to enroll in various social programs, report violations of rights, and find various support services for women in situations of violence and vulnerability.
